My skills

My creative skills & experiences.

• As a Blender 3D Artist with an MCA background, I combine artistic creativity and technical expertise to create high-quality 3D models, animations, and visual effects. My skills include hard surface and organic modeling, sculpting, UV mapping, and procedural geometry. I excel in PBR texturing and shading using Substance Painter and Blender’s node system, ensuring realistic and optimized assets for games and animations.

• I specialize in rigging and animation, bringing characters to life with fluid motion. My expertise in lighting and rendering with Cycles and Eevee enables me to produce cinematic-quality visuals. I also work with VFX and simulations such as cloth, fire, smoke, and particles, enhancing realism in scenes.

• With a strong programming foundation, I leverage Python scripting to automate workflows and create custom Blender tools. My knowledge of shader programming (GLSL, HLSL) and real-time rendering allows me to optimize assets for Unreal Engine and Unity.

• I am detail-oriented, efficient in time management, and excel in problem-solving. Passionate about learning, I continuously explore new 3D tools and technologies to stay ahead in the industry.
